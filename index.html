About Us

Why We Formed a Relay For Life Team Is for friends and family that no longer have the opportunity to fight for a cure. We feel anything we can do to fight this terrible disease we need to be helping. There are too many family and friends in our lives that have lost their battle with cancer and we walk for them, the survivors, and a CURE!!! We would like to show respect to the people who have watched a love one die, the people who are caring for a child, parent, spouse, or grandparent that is in misery and depending on them because of their cancer progression! We relay because too many of us have been in their shoes and it is a heart wrenching experience to watch someone you love lose themselves to this incurable disease. We relay to honor our loved ones who are fighting this disease and in memory of our loved ones who have lost their lives to Cancer. Cancer is ugly and working together for a cure is beautiful! We CAN beat Cancer... hopefully sooner than later! God Bless you, and be with you in whatever battle you are going through! We relay to remember our loved ones lost to cancer and for the loved ones who battle cancer and all the others along the way. We are made up of some amazing people! We all relay for a reason that matters to us! We relay because we hope to find a cure for our children or grandchildren! Some of us do it for all of these reasons! Some just to have fun, some just for support! No matter why, together we can make a difference!

About Relay For Life

At Relay For Life events, communities across the globe come together to honor cancer survivors, remember loved ones lost, and fight back against a disease that has already taken too much. The funds you raise truly make a difference in the fight against cancer. Just ask one of the nearly 14 million cancer survivors who will celebrate another birthday this year!
